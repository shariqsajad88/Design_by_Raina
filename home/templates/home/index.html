About Us

At Design By Raina, we believe that great design transforms spaces and enriches lives. Our passion lies in creating interiors that reflect your unique personality, lifestyle, and aspirations—spaces that feel like home the moment you walk in.

Our Philosophy

We approach every project with creativity, functionality, and attention to detail. By listening closely to our clients, we craft bespoke interiors that are both beautiful and practical.

Who We Are

Founded by Saman Raina, our studio is a team of passionate designers experienced in residential and commercial design. Our work has been featured in top publications and is trusted by clients across the region.

What We Offer
  • • Personalized interior design consultations
  • • Full-service project management from concept to completion
  • • Space planning, color consultation, and material selection
  • • Sourcing of unique furnishings and décor
Our Process

We make the design journey seamless and inspiring. From the first consultation to the final reveal, we guide you every step of the way to ensure your vision becomes a reality—on time and within budget.

Why Choose Us?

Clients love our collaborative spirit, transparent communication, and commitment to excellence. We treat each project as a partnership and design with heart, purpose, and precision.

Our Services

At Design By Raina, we offer a comprehensive range of interior design services tailored to meet your unique needs and style. Whether you're redesigning a single room or transforming an entire property, our expert team is here to bring your vision to life with creativity and precision.

Residential Design

Beautiful and comfortable living spaces designed for your lifestyle and personality. We create homes that tell your story.

Commercial Design

Modern and productive environments tailored for your business needs. Spaces that enhance brand identity and experience.

Renovation & Remodeling

Transform your current space with expert renovation services. Breathe new life into your existing property.

  • 1. Interior Design Consultation

    Personalized sessions to understand your style and goals. We offer expert guidance on layout, color schemes, lighting, and materials.

  • 2. Space Planning & Layout Design

    Optimizing your space for functionality and flow, making every inch both beautiful and useful.

  • 3. Custom Residential Design

    From cozy apartments to luxury homes, we design interiors that match your personality and lifestyle.

  • 4. Commercial Interior Design

    Designing inspiring spaces for offices, retail, hospitality, and more to enhance brand experience and customer appeal.

  • 5. Furniture & Decor Selection

    Handpicked furniture, fixtures, and accessories to complete your vision with elegance and uniqueness.

  • 6. Color Consultation

    Expert help in selecting color palettes that set the perfect mood and blend beautifully with your surroundings.

  • 7. Project Management

    We manage every detail from planning to execution—budgets, timelines, contractors—so you don't have to.

  • 8. Renovation & Remodeling Design

    Innovative redesign solutions that refresh and upgrade your current spaces with value-driven transformations.
